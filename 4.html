Anne Amie

Ever since her debut in "Kochavva Paulo Ayyappa Coelho", Anne Amie has been soaring through the music industry. Having crooned for recent releases "Uyiril Thodum" for Kumbalangi Nights, "Vicharamo" for and "Adhyam Thammil" for June, Anne has sung a plethora of renowned songs. A native of Thrissur and schooled in Dubai, Anne has been an ardent singer from a young age well into her college days, where she was introduced to various genres of music through her band.

With a velvety voice and bubbly personality she was a finalist in Idea star singer season 2, was featured in Amrita TV's 'Autumn leaf show' and holds multiple awards to her name. To name but a few, she had Won ‘Upcoming Female Vocalist of the Year' in Malayalam at Mirchi Music Awards 2017 and ‘Best Singer - Female’ at NANA Film Awards 2018. Her career skyrocketed with the track 'Aaraaro’ from Anjali Menon’s Koode, composed by Raghu Dixit, in which her exceptional voice won the hearts of her ever-growing fanbase. This March 24, be there at Ragam 19 to witness a riveting performance from Anne Amie.
